## Decisions Made

- Kept `whatsapp.to` as the compatibility seed source instead of introducing a separate subscriber config list.
- Used a unique `(channel, recipient)` guard so duplicate seed or duplicate admin-managed channels cannot fan out twice.
- Kept status values as strings/constants, avoiding a larger subscription state machine for v1.
