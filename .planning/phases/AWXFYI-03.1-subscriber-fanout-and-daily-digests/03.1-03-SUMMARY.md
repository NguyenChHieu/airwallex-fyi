## Accomplishments

- Added compact multi-post digest formatting with headline, two bullets, why-it-matters, and direct links.
- Added exact no-change message formatting: `Airwallex FYI: No new public Blog or Newsroom updates today.`
- Added `DailyDigestService` to fan out to active WhatsApp subscriber channels with local-date duplicate checks.
- Persisted delivery status and digest-post links per subscriber channel, while continuing after individual channel failures.
- Refactored the notifier boundary so digest payloads do not need fake `PostRecord` IDs.
- Updated dry-run and Twilio tests to verify payload sending without per-post notification attempt persistence.
